Subsidiary Intelligence Gathering Agent

An intelligent command-line application to extract specific corporate intelligence from various LLMs, focusing on verifiable data from the model's internal knowledge base.

Core Capabilities

SIGA is designed with a focus on flexibility, robustness, and traceability.

Targeted Information Extraction

Focuses specifically on gathering subsidiary names, locations, and data sources, minimizing noise and maximizing relevance.

Flexible LLM Integration

Supports OpenAI, Google AI (Gemini), and local Ollama models, allowing users to choose the best tool for their needs and budget.

Dynamic Model & Prompt Selection

Interactively lists available models from the chosen provider and allows selection from versioned, externalized prompt templates.

Robust Output & Traceability

Generates a structured Excel file with linked summary/detail sheets and saves raw JSON responses for complete auditability.

Error Handling & Resumption

Features a 1-minute timeout for AI calls and skips failed entries during batch processing to ensure continuous operation.

Modern Agentic Design

Employs a modular architecture, abstract interfaces, and sophisticated prompt engineering to create an extensible and reliable agent.
